Flash Claudeq

Install the firmware on your Waveshare ESP32-S3-Touch-LCD-3.49 — straight from this page. Nothing to download.

  1. Use Chrome or Edge on a desktop (they speak WebSerial).
  2. Plug the device into a USB-C port on your computer.
  3. Click Install, pick the serial port, and let it flash (~30 s).
Your browser can't flash over USB — open this page in desktop Chrome or Edge. Serial access was blocked. Reload and allow the port when prompted.

After flashing, the device boots into WiFi setup: on your phone join the Claudeq-setup hotspot and enter your home WiFi. Then install the bridge with brew install Positronico/tap/claudeq and run claudeq in any project. See the full guide.
